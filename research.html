I am a Ph.D. student in Information Technology at the George Mason University.

My research mainly interests in HyperDimensional Computing (HDC). HDC is new type of brain-inspired neural network, which has demonstrated its superiority in different machine learning (ML) tasks in terms of robustness, scalability, and high energy efficiency. The main concept of HDC is to formulate a space with a set of high- dimension orthogonal vectors, so ML tasks are performed in such a space. The detail please see my publications in my C.V..

My past research interests in Non-Volatile Memories (NVM), which is is a type of computer memory that can retain stored information even after power is removed. We made some research on the data management scheme for some specific environment which is based on NVM.
