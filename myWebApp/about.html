About

This work presents an interactive map of the top universities in the world. The online map we proposed can show the universities according to the locations of universities. Additionally, the introduction to every university will be highlighted when the mouse clicks the corresponding marker. The cross filter is being applied to select the data according to the different requirements. Furthermore, the bar chart, pie chart and histogram will display the various types of data with a cross-filtering function, and the detailed datasets will appear as a table below the map for better understanding and visualization.
